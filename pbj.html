Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a peanut butter and jelly sandwich. Not much to it. Recipe is literally in the name.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice
  4. Bring both slices together
