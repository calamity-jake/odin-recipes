Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A simple grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Heat
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Spread butter on two slices of bread
  2. Put two slices of cheese between bread
  3. Bring both slices of bread together
  4. Heat in pan until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted
  5. Bam! You're done
